Ottawa Icebreaker Soccer Tournament

Hosted by the Ottawa Internationals Soccer Club in Ottawa - Ontario - Canada

About us

The Ottawa Internationals Soccer Club is very pleased to cordially invite your team to participate in the 28th Edition of the "Icebreaker" Soccer Festivals and Tournaments.

The Icebreaker is split over two week-ends, with the Girls' Tournaments and Festivals being held May 24-25, 2025 and the Boys' Tournaments and Festivals being held the following week-end of May 31 to June 1, 2025.
