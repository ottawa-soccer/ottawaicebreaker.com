Hotels and accommodations

Hudson Travel Group (HTG) is the Exclusive and Official Travel Partner for the Icebreaker Soccer Tournaments and Festivals. One of the criteria for acceptance into the Icebreaker will be that your hotel accommodations have been secured through Hudson Travel Group.

  • Hudson Travel Group provides the on-line registration and team communication software that we need and makes it easier for our guest teams to secure accommodations.
  • HTG secures blocks of rooms at various hotels within our tournament's geographic area at very attractive rates.
  • In addition, due to our long-standing relationship with HTG, their staff are well aware of the location of our fields and can set you up at a hotel that is closer to your game fields. So, please be sure to contact HTG early to secure your accommodations.

Important!

Book your accommodations as early as possible for the Girls' tournament. The National Capital Marathon and Race Week-end happen the same weekend and rooms are harder to find.
