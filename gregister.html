Girls Premiere Teams

U-9, U-10, U-11 and U-12 Festivals

  • "District Development Tier 1" Teams with better skilled or more experienced players
  • Soccer Academy Alliance of Canada (SAAC) Festival-aged teams will, whenever possible, play up by one year in the Festival's Ice Pile Divisions.

U-13 to U-17/18 Divisions

These are for

  • OS Regional (Formerly "Level #3") teams
  • Quebec "AA" Level teams
  • Ontario OPDL (Formerly "Level #1") and Quebec "AAA" teams that are "playing up" by a year).
  • Any team playing at the highest level within an Ontario Regional or a Multi-Jurisdictional league or a Quebec-based league, and/or Teams playing at the highest level within their own League,
  • Ontario District Competitive (Formerly "Level #4") and Quebec "A" Teams that qualify to play in the Snow Bank but which ask to play in the Ice Pile may be allowed to play in the Ice Pile.

OPDL, LSEQ, US ODP, Super-Y and Soccer Academy Alliance of Canada (SAAC) teams must play in the Ice Pile AND in an age group that is one year older than the age group in which they are registered for play in their outdoor League.

All teams must play in an outdoor League this coming summer.

Girls District Teams

The U-9, U-10, U-11 and U-12 Festivals are for District Development Tier #2 and Tier #3 Teams with lesser skilled or less experienced players than are on Tier #1 teams.

The U-13 to U-17/18 Snow Banks include:

  • OS District Competitive (Formerly "Level #4", "Level #5" and "District" teams,
  • Quebec "A" Level teams,
  • Club Teams playing in an Ontario District League,
  • Recreation level teams that are "carded" to play "Competitively", and
  • Ontario Regional (Formerly "Level #3") and Quebec "AA" Ice Pile-qualified teams that are "playing up" by at least one year.

Ontario District Competitive (Formerly "Level #4") and Quebec "A" Teams that qualify to play in the Snow Bank may ask to play in the Ice Pile.

All teams must play in an outdoor League this coming summer.
