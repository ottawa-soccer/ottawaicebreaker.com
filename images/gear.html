Richard Shorty (the artist)

Richard Shorty is an indigenous artist born in Whitehorse, Yukon Territory and belongs to the Northern Tuchone Tribe. He is a self-taught artist whose crest is Crow. Richard is one of the most sought-after Native artists, with works in many galleries. He is very versatile, working on drums, paddles, masks, rattles in addition to his paintings. His pieces are collected internationally.

Mr Shorty has graciously allowed us to use his image of the wolverine as the symbol for the 25th anniversary of the Icebreaker tournament.

His artwork can be found on the Art Country Canada website at http://www.artcountrycanada.com/shorty.htm
