Team and Player Entry Registration
Team and player registration will be done at the field at least 1 hour prior to your first game.

Player Registration Information Instructions
The instructions below apply to the production of Game Sheets and Bench Staff Sheets. A Game Sheet will serve as your team's Official Ice Breaker Tournament Player Roster.

  • Please download the Game Sheet file to your computer and open the PDF.
  • Once completed please print the file. (if you cannot complete the form digitally, please print the form and fill it out by hand and make the required number of copies)
  • Festival Teams – Print 6 copies of the game sheet.
  • Tournament Teams – Print 10 copies of the game sheet.
  • Please download and complete one (1) copy of the Bench Staff List and submit it when you register your team.
  • A Travel Permit is required by all out-of-district teams. If your players' books contain a district stamp other from than the Eastern Ontario District Soccer Association (EODSA), you require a Travel Permit - even if you play in the Eastern Ontario Soccer League (EOSL). Please email us your travel permit once it is approved.
  • Quebec teams: Please remember that you need two (2) stamps of approval on your Travel Permits (one from your district and one from the FSQ).

Registration will take place at the field a minimum of 1 hour prior to your first game. There will be a registration tent at each venue.
All players must be present and receive a hand stamp before they can play in the festival or tournament.

U-9 to U-12 teams

You will need to submit the following when you register:

  • A copy of your Official Ontario Soccer Team Roster or an Official Quebec Soccer Federation Team roster or a typed list of your team's Registered Players on your Soccer Club's letterhead signed by your club verifying that all players are registered with your provincial association. 
  • 6 Ice Breaker game sheets
  • 1 team bench list
  • Travel permit if applicable
  • All of your players

Every player must be present at registration and will receive a hand stamp. If a player is not available when your team is registered that player must register with the tournament before playing.

U-13 and older teams
You will need to submit the following when you register:

  • A copy of your Official Ontario Soccer Team Roster or an Official Quebec Soccer Federation Team roster.
  • 10 Ice Breaker game sheets
  • 1 team bench list
  • Travel permit if applicable
  • Player cards (actual cards or digital cards
  • All of your players

A card check will be performed during team registration. Every player must be present at registration and will receive a hand stamp. If a player is not available when your team is registered that player must register with the tournament before playing.
