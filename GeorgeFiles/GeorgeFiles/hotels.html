HOTEL AND ACCOMMODATION

    The success of our tournament relies heavily on the combination and co-operation of great volunteers and the partnerships that have been established with our sponsors. Hudson Travel Group is the exclusive travel partner for the Icebreaker Soccer Tournament.

    Effective immediately, one of the criteria for acceptance into our tournament will be that your hotel accommodations have been secured through Hudson Travel Group.



    HTG has blocks of rooms put aside at very attractive rates at all of the participating host hotels. So, please be sure to contact HTG early to secure your accommodations.



    This is especially important to do if you will be attending the Girls' Icebreaker as that particular week-end is also when the National Capital Marathon and Race Week-end is held.



    We thank you in advance for your support.
