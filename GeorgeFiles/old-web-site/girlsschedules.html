 INSTRUCTIONS

  • Game Sheets - The Icebreaker Tournaments use their own on-line "fillable" forms for Games Sheets, The Discipline Log and the Bench Staff Sheet. These simple-to-use forms are available to you by using the "Registrations and Forms" link on this site. Please follow the instructions at that link page and remember to use the "Print Button" that appears on the Game Sheet (and do NOT use "Control P" or any other method to print the documents). This process prints out eleven copies of your game sheet and one copy of your team's Discipline Log automatically.
  • Team Bench Staff List - You will also need to fill in, print and submit one (1) copy of the on-line Team Bench Staff List.
  • Travel Permit - A Travel Permit is required by all out-of-district teams. If your players' books or cards contain a district stamp other than the "EODSA" stamp, you require a Travel Permit. This rule applies even if your team plays in the ERSL. Please either e-mail it to icebreaker@ottawasoccer.com or fax it to 613-237-1890 before you arrive at registration. ALL TEAMS REGISTERED IN QUEBEC REQUIRE TWO (2) APPROVAL STAMPS ON THEIR TRAVEL PERMIT. Thanks.
  • Player Books/Cards - For U-13 to U-18 teams: At registration, you will need to have your player books or cards duly completed by your district for the 2015 outdoor season. Please note that we are not permitted to accept your team's OSA Team Roster as the only proof that your team or the individual players are duly registered. WE ARE ALSO NOT PERMITTED TO ACCEPT AS PROOF OF A PLAYER'S IDENTITY ANY OTHER PHOTO IDENTIFICATION OTHER THAN THE PLAYER'S CURRENTLY APPROVED BOOK OR CARD. You must have a properly certified book or card GU14_Snow_7Team_Ungava_2016.htmlfor each player.
  • For U-9 to U-12 Teams: The Icebreaker is not requiring teams to have a card or a passport for each of their players. Instead, each team will need to provide an Official Roster or Team List on the letterhead of the Club for which the players are registered and this List is to be signed by the Club's President, Registrar or Head Coach whose signature verifies that each listed player is registered with that Club for the 2015 Summer season and that each player is covered by the OSA's or the QSF's Insurance Policy.
  • Rules - It is recommended that you download a copy of the Tournament Rules (Long Version) for your reference during the Icebreaker.
Schedules and Changes - Tournament Schedules will be posted as soon as possible and each team will be notified by e-mail when they are available and when or if it has changed for your team's Pool. We will release the first edition of the schedules on May 2, 2014.
  1. Click on your Age Division and Pool to view your schedule.
  2. Since some Age Divisions and Pools will have more than one schedule on the page, PLEASE SCROLL ALL THE WAY DOWN THE PAGE TO SEE ALL OF THE TEAMS' SCHEDULES. Please note that groups of 12 or more teams have more than one schedule on one page.
NOTE:
YOU MUST BE REGISTERED IN THE CORRECT POOL BASED UPON THE GROUPING AND DEFINITION POSTED ON THE "INFORMATION" PAGE OF THIS WEB-SITE. IF, AT REGISTRATION, WE FOUND OUT THAT YOU ARE IN THE WRONG POOL, WE WILL HAVE NO OPTION BUT TO NOT ALLOW YOUR TEAM TO PARTICIPATE IN THE TOURNAMENT AND NO REFUND WILL BE PROVIDED. THIS RULE WILL BE IMPLEMENTED SO AS TO MAKE THE POOLS AS EQUITABLE AND AS FAIR AS IS POSSIBLE.
