2016 TEAM AND PLAYER ENTRY REGISTRATION

The on-line team and player registration links are at the bottom of this page. The process has been stream-lined this year by the fact that you will no longer be prompted to provide player information at the time that you register your team. Instead, in mid-May we will inform you by e-mail how to supply this information on a different electronic form and we will collect that information during your team's players' registration on the Thursday night before your tournament starts or on the Saturday or Sunday morning on which your team's Tournament or Festival starts.

PLAYER INFORMATION INSTRUCTIONS



The instructions below apply to the production of Game Sheets (one of which sheets will serve as your team's Official Icebreaker Tournament Player Roster), Discipline Logs and Bench Staff Sheets. The document links below WILL BE ACTIVATED on THURSDAY, MAY 19, 2016 and the Point-of-Contact for all accepted teams will be notified by e-mail regarding teh fact that the documents are "alive" and how to fill in and provide us the needed information.
  • Please open the "Fillable pdf" file named "Game Sheet and Team Discipline Log" (below). If you cannot open the file, you probably need a newer version of Adobe Reader. To get a free copy of this program, click on the red square beside the documents' titles below. This link will take you to the Adobe site, where you can download the latest version of Adobe Reader.
    Although the document that you will see does NOT look like a Game Sheet or a Team Discipline Log, simply fill in the required and requested information.
    Once all of the info has been entered and you are ready to print the documents,    PRESS THE PRINT BUTTON AT THE BOTTOM OF THE PAGE THAT YOU HAVE JUST FILLED IN. The program will automatically insert the inputted information into the appropriate fields of the Game Sheet and the Team Discipline Log. The program will automatically print ten (10) Game Sheets and one (1) Team Discipline Log.
    Do NOT press "Control-P" or any other printing or printer icon because pushing eirther of those kinds of command buttons will cause you to print just the page on the screen which is neither a Game Sheet nor a Team Discipline Log - and such a page is not useful to you as a guest team or to us as hosting administrators.
  • Please download and complete one (1) copy of the Bench Staff List and submit it when you register your team.
  • A Travel Permit is required by ALL out-of-district teams. Basically, if your players' books contain a district stamp other from than the Eastern Ontario District Soccer Association (EODSA), you require a Travel Permit - even if you play in the East Region Soccer League (ERSL). Please FAX the Travel Permit in advance to 613-237-1890 or scan and e-mail it to "icebreaker@ottawasoccer.com". Thanks. Quebec teams: Please note that you need TWO (2) stamps of approval on your Travel Permits.
  • Registration will be held from 6:00 pm until 9:00 pm on Thursday May 26, 2016 (for the Girls' Icebreaker) and on June 2, 2016 for the Boys' Icebreaker. Registration will be in the hallway outside of the new gym at Ecole Secondaire Franco-Cite (923 Smyth Road).



  • FOR U-9 TO U-12 TEAMS: At Player Registration, you will NOT need to have any 2016 player cards (or books). Instead, you will need to provide a typed list of your team's Registered Players on YOUR SOCCER CLUB'S LETTERHEAD. In addition, you will note that on the Icebreaker Game Sheets, WE ARE REQUIRING TWO SIGNATURES. The first is the signature of a Club Executive Member (Club Registrar, President or Vice-President) whose signature confirms that all players whose names appear on the Icebreaker Game Sheet are duly and properly registered with their Provincial Soccer Association or Federation for 2016; and the second signature is that of the team's Coach whose signature confirms that he or she will only allow the above-listed players to participate in any game in the Icebreaker Festival. we are requiring or you can provide an Official Ontario Soccer Association Team Roster or an Official Quebec Soccer Federation Team roster or a 2016 Game-used Game Sheet that lists the names and registration numbers of your players.

  • FOR ALL U-13 AND OLDER TEAMS: At Player Registration, you will need to have your players' cards (or books); and in addition, you will need to provide either a typed list of the names of your team's Registered Players on YOUR SOCCER CLUB'S LETTERHEAD or you can provide an Official Ontario Soccer Association Team Roster or an Official Quebec Soccer Federation Team Roster or an Official Team Roster properly completed and approved by your District Association. You will also need to show your team's Official OSA Team Roster AND THE TEAM ROSTER FOR THE USUAL OUTDOOR TEAM FOR EACH GUEST PLAYER. Please note that the OSA Roster DOES NOT replace any player's missing card or book. You must have both the cards or books AND one of the Roster documents listed above. Although we will NOT keep a copy of any OSA or QSF-issued Official Roster, you will need to keep it on hand in case any player eligibility questions (etc.) arise during the tournament.
