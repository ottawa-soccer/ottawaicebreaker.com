Accommodations and reservations - Girl's tournament

Accommodations are available at the following facilities for the weekend of the girl's tournament.

  • Carleton University residences
  • Ottawa University residences
  • Algonquin College residences

Free wireless internet is available and a complimentary hot and cold breakfast buffet is included your stay. Breakfast is served between 6:30 am and 9:00 am in the Fresh Food Company Cafeteria which is located on the 3rd floor of Residence Commons.

Carleton University is 10-15 minutes from the majority of the Icebreaker's fields.

This "Block Booking" has a limited duration and all unbooked rooms will be put back into Carleton's Public Rental Pool as of April 27, 2016.

Algonquin College residences

Our signature style fully-furnished 2 bedroom suites come complete with two separate bedrooms, each with a double bed, work space (desk and chair), television and telephone. Also included is a kitchenette with microwave, refrigerator and dining area, plus a three-piece private washroom. At Residence & Conference Centre - Ottawa West there are absolutely no hidden fees. Our rates already include FREE parking, high speed Internet, local telephone calls, as well as all the guest amenities that you would expect from a pricier hotel such as toiletries, linens and housekeeping service.

Algonquin's Residence building is just a 10-15 minute drive from the majority of the Icebreaker's fields. In the suburb of Nepean, we are just minutes away from local attractions like the Nepean Sportsplex, Centrepointe Theatre, Canadian Tire Centre and College Square.

We are happy to offer you a discounted group rate when booking 10 rooms or more of $110.00 per night per suite, based on double occupancy before taxes (13%) and based on a two night minimum stay. Please state that you are with the "Icebreaker Tournament" or quote Group Booking Number 411822 when reserving your rooms. Thanks.
