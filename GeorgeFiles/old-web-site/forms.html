FORMS


2017 TEAM AND PLAYER ENTRY REGISTRATION

The on-line team and player registration links are at the bottom of this page. The process has been shortened for all registering teams this year by the fact that you will no longer be prompted to provide player information at the time that you register your team. Instead, in mid-May we will inform you by e-mail how to supply this information on a different electronic form and we will collect that information during your team's players' registration on the Thursday night before your tournament starts or on the Saturday or Sunday morning on which your team's Tournament or Festival starts.

PLAYER REGISTRATION INFORMATION INSTRUCTIONS



The instructions below apply to the production of Game Sheets (one of which sheets will serve as your team's Official Icebreaker Tournament Player Roster), Discipline Logs and Bench Staff Sheets. The document links below WILL BE activated in mid-May or about a week before the Girls' Icebreaker happens; and the Point-of-Contact for all accepted teams will be notified by e-mail regarding the fact that the documents are "alive" and how to fill in and provide us the needed information.
  • Please DOWNLOAD (i.e. DO NOT TRY TO FILL IN THESE DOCUMENTS ON YOUR BROWSER) and open the "Fillable pdf" file named "Game Sheet and Discipline Log" (below). Please note that you cannot complete this form inside a web browser. The file must be downloaded to your desktop or other file location and opened using Adobe Acrobat Reader.
  • To download from Internet Explorer: right click on the link and select "save target as". This will prompt you to save the file to a location on your computer.
  • To download from Chrome and Firefox: right click on the link and select "save link as". This will prompt you to save the file to a location on your computer.
  • If you cannot open the file, you probably need a newer version of Adobe Reader. To get a free copy of this program, click on the red square beside the documents' titles below. This link will take you to the Adobe site, where you can download the latest version of Adobe Acrobat Reader.

    Although the document that you will see does NOT look like a Game Sheet or a Team Discipline Log, simply fill in the required and requested information.
    Once all of the info has been entered and you are ready to print the documents,     PRESS THE "PRINT" BUTTON AT THE BOTTOM OF THE PAGE THAT YOU HAVE JUST FILLED IN. The program will automatically insert the inputted information into the appropriate fields of the Game Sheet and the Team Discipline Log. The program will automatically print 1 Roster, 6 to 12 Game Sheets and 1 Team Discipline Log.
    Do NOT press "Control-P" or any other printing or printer icon because pushing either of those kinds of command buttons will cause you to print just the page on the screen which is neither a Game Sheet nor a Team Discipline Log - and such a page is not useful to you as a guest team or to us as hosting administrators.

  • Please download and complete one (1) copy of the Bench Staff List and submit it when you register your team.

  • A Travel Permit is required by ALL out-of-district teams. Basically, if your players' books contain a district stamp other from than the Eastern Ontario District Soccer Association (EODSA), you require a Travel Permit - even if you play in the East Region Soccer League (ERSL). Please FAX the Travel Permit in advance to 613-237-1890 or scan and e-mail it to "icebreaker@ottawasoccer.com". Thanks.

    Quebec teams: Please remember that you need TWO (2) stamps of approval on your Travel Permits.

  • Registration will be held from 6:00 pm until 9:00 pm on Thursday May 25, 2017 for the Girls' Icebreaker; and on June 1, 2017 for the Boys' Icebreaker.

    Registration will be in the hallway outside of the new gym at Ecole Secondaire Franco-Cite (623 Smyth Road). This gym is at the back of the school and right in front of the artificial turf soccer and football field. It is best to park in the parking lot to the East of the building as the lot is larger than the West lot and it is closer to the registration hallway.


  • FOR U-9 TO U-12 TEAMS: At Player Registration, you will NOT need to have any 2017 player cards (or books). Instead, you will need to provide a typed list of your team's Registered Players on YOUR SOCCER CLUB'S LETTERHEAD. In addition, you will note that on the Icebreaker Game Sheets, WE ARE REQUIRING TWO SIGNATURES. The first is the signature of a Club Executive Member (Club Registrar, President or Vice-President) whose signature confirms that all players whose names appear on the Icebreaker Game Sheet are duly and properly registered with their Provincial Soccer Association or Federation for 2017; and the second signature is that of the team's Coach whose signature confirms that he or she will only allow the above-listed players to participate in any game in the Icebreaker Festival. we are requiring or you can provide an Official Ontario Soccer Association Team Roster or an Official Quebec Soccer Federation Team roster or a 2016 Game-used Game Sheet that lists the names and registration numbers of your players.

  • FOR ALL U-13 AND OLDER TEAMS: At Player Registration, you will need to have your players' cards (or books); and in addition, you will need to provide either a typed list of the names of your team's Registered Players on YOUR SOCCER CLUB'S LETTERHEAD or you can provide an Official Ontario Soccer Association Team Roster or an Official Quebec Soccer Federation Team Roster or an Official Team Roster properly completed and approved by your District Association.

    You will also need to show your team's Official OSA Team Roster (or QSF or District Association-provided or approved Official Roster) AND THE TEAM ROSTER FOR THE USUAL OUTDOOR TEAM FOR EACH GUEST PLAYER. Please note that the OSA Roster DOES NOT replace any player's missing card or book. You must have BOTH the cards or books AND one of the Roster documents listed above.

    Although we will NOT keep a copy of any OSA or QSF-issued Official Roster, you will need to keep it on hand in case any player eligibility questions (etc.) arise during the tournament.
