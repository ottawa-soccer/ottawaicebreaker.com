 INSTRUCTIONS

  • You are required to download from the web site and complete the game sheet and have 10 copies on hand when you register your team. Game sheet
  • You are required to download and complete two copies of the discipline report and submit them when you register. Discipline report
  • You are required to submit one copy of the Bench list. Bench list
  • Travel permit required for all out of district teams. Basically if your player's book contain a district stamp other than the EODSA, you require a travel permit. Even if you play in the ERSL. FAX it 613 237-1890 before you arrive
  • At registration you are required to have your player books duly completed by your district for the 2006 outdoor season. The OSA team roster will not be acceptable as the only proof the team is registered. You must have the books.
  • Download a set of the tournament rules ( Long Version) for your reference.
Preliminary Tournament Schedules to be posted soon.
  1. Click on your group to view your schedule.
  2. Some groups will have more than one schedule on the page, Scroll down to see all the schedules. You will note that groups of 12 16 and 20 have more than one schedule on one page.
NOTE:
YOU MUST BE REGISTERED IN THE CORRECT POOL BASED ON THE GROUPING AND DEFINITION POSTED ON THE SITE. IF, AT REGISTRATION, WE FOUND OUT THAT YOU ARE IN THE WRONG POOL YOU WILL NOT BE ALLOWED TO PARTICIPATE IN THE TOURNAMENT AND NO REFUND WILL BE PROVIDED.
