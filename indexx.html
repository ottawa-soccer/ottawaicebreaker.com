News

Schedule changes require that all Festivals for Tier 1 Teams (Ice Pile Divisions) in the U-9 to U-12 age groups be played on Saturday and Tier 2 and Tier 3 Festvals (Snow Bank Divisions) will be played on Sunday.

Ontario Soccer (OS) has again already approved the participation of out-of-province teams to play in the U-9 to U-12 Festivals.

The U-9, U-10, U-11 and U-12 Festivals will again be one-day events with four (4) short games for each team.
